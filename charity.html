Integniklyde Productions

We strive to sell eco-friendly products that are not only unique but also functional. With these products, we aim to provide goods that help the environment with integrity. Integniklyde Productions donates a percentage of its profits to ANCOP.

What is ANCOP?

Ancop stands for ANswering the Cry of the Poor. This charity aims to provide help for children in need. Its core values are Pro-God, Pro-Family, Pro-Life, and Pro-Poor, with the key principle that Christ is the center of the work and must be proclaimed both in word and deed.
